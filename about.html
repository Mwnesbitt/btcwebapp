What is this site?

This service allows you to view balances associated with any bitcoin address.

You can load the addresses that you want to view and conveniently access them by logging in to your account, meaning you only have to enter an address once in order to track it.

While watch-only wallet applications exist, this site can be accessed through a browser from anywhere (as long as you remember your username and password.) You also don't have to worry about malware impersonating your wallet or accidentally crossing your wires and exposing your private keys in your watch-only environment.
